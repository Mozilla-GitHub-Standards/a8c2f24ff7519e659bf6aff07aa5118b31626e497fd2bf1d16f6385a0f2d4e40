Other Opportunities

You don't have to build your own Firefox Distribution to partner with Mozilla, check out more about how you can partner with us.

Tiles

 The Firefox new tab page now includes Tiles, providing users easy access to interesting, relevant content, whether it’s the sites they visit most or new sites for them to discover. Learn more about Tiles and contact us to get involved!
